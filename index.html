I am a Ph.D. student at POSTECH. I build photorealistic and social world simulations for the next generation of physical AI and global communication. Specifically, I solve challenging inverse problems of synthesizing photorealistic 3D/4D digital twins, including humans, scenes, objects, using real-time graphics and generative AI.

I am an incoming research scientist intern at NVIDIA Real-Time Graphics Research. I will also be re-joining Meta Codec Avatars Lab as a research scientist intern. I was a research scientist intern at Meta Codec Avatars Lab, and a visiting Ph.D. researcher at the Real Virtual Humans Group at the Univ. of Tübingen.
