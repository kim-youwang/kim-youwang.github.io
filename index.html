Research Interests

I am interested in 3D/4D virtual humans, animals and objects. My goal is to enrich a virtual world through efficient, realistic and creative virtual humans. Currently, I'm focusing on below topics, but not limited to.

  • Realistic 3D/4D human and animal generation for virtual world.
  • Accurate 3D pose and shape reconstruction of humans and animals.
